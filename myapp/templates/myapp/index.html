About The Coding Pirate's

Welcome to The Coding Pirate's, a collective of enthusiastic gamers and coders passionate about both gaming and technology. Our team includes four members: Atharv, Shivansh, Suraj, and Mayuresh. Each of us brings unique skills and perspectives to the table. Atharv, Shivansh, and Suraj are avid gamers, diving into different genres and trends, while Mayuresh contributes his expertise in coding as our non-gamer with a fresh perspective.

Our project, created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with Django linking it all together, is a dedicated platform for gaming blogs. Here, we cover everything from game reviews to insights into the latest releases, and we share our own experiences and tips to enhance your gaming journey. This platform is designed to be interactive, user-friendly, and full of the insights every gamer craves.

Join us as we explore the world of gaming with our passion for coding. Whether you're here for the gaming insights or the tech behind it, The Coding Pirate's welcomes you aboard!
